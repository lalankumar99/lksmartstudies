Bihar Polytechnic Counselling 2026: The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has officially started the online counselling and choice filling process for the Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE - PE) 2026. Candidates who have successfully qualified for the exam and secured a rank can now participate in the counselling process to get admission into various government and private polytechnic colleges across Bihar.

In this detailed article by LK SMART STUDIES, we have provided complete information regarding the registration process, online choice filling, required documents, and important dates for Bihar Polytechnic Counselling 2026.

campaign Update: The link for online registration and choice filling is now active. Candidates are advised to fill maximum choices of colleges and branches to increase their chances of seat allotment.

file_present Required Documents for Document Verification

Once a seat is allotted, candidates must report to their respective nodal centers with the original copies and 2 sets of self-attested photocopies of the following documents:

  • DCECE (PE) 2026 Rank Card / Merit List
  • DCECE 2026 Admit Card
  • Part-A and Part-B of online application form (Hard copy)
  • Class 10th (Matriculation) Admit Card, Marksheet, and Passing Certificate
  • Residential Certificate (Niwas Praman Patra)
  • Caste Certificate (Jati Praman Patra) - If applicable
  • Income Certificate (Aay Praman Patra) - For EWS/TFW candidates
  • Character Certificate from last attended school/college
  • Aadhar Card
  • 6 Passport Size Photographs (Same as pasted on the Admit Card)
  • Provisional Allotment Order (3 Copies)
  • Verification Slip (Jaanch Parchi) - 2 Copies

format_list_numbered Step-by-Step Online Choice Filling Process

Follow these easy steps to successfully register and fill your choices for Bihar Polytechnic Counselling 2026:

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to the official BCECEB website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Click on Counselling Link: Look for the "Online Counselling Portal of DCECE (PE)-2026" in the latest updates section.
  3. New Registration: Click on "New Candidate Registration". Agree to the terms and enter your Roll Number, Candidate Name, Date of Birth, and Security Pin.
  4. Create Password: Create a strong password. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number and email. Verify it.
  5. Login: Go back and log in using your Roll Number and the newly created Password.
  6. Fill Choices: Click on "Choice Filling". Here you will see a list of available institutes and branches. Add your preferred colleges and trades (e.g., Civil, Electrical, Mechanical) to the right side.
  7. Arrange Preferences: Use the Up/Down buttons to arrange your choices in order of highest priority to lowest priority.
  8. Save and Lock Choices: Once satisfied, click on "Save and Continue". Finally, click on "Lock Choices". (Note: Choices once locked cannot be changed).
  9. Print Hard Copy: Print the locked choices list for future reference during document verification.
