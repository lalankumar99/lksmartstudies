LNMU UG Admission 2026–30 Overview

Lalit Narayan Mithila University (LNMU) offers online admission for a 4-year undergraduate degree program under the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) / NEP 2020. Students who have passed their Class 12th board examinations are eligible to fill out the online admission form.

Eligibility Criteria

To apply for the LNMU UG Admission 2026-30, candidates must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Must have passed the Intermediate (Class 12th) exam from a recognized board (BSEB, CBSE, ICSE, etc.).
  • For Honours Course: A minimum of 45% marks in the subject concerned in the Class 12th examination.
  • For General/Pass Course: A minimum of 33% overall marks in the Class 12th examination.
  • Students can choose their Honours subject based on their stream and marks obtained in 12th.

Required Documents

Keep scanned copies of the following documents ready before starting the online application:

  1. Class 10th Marksheet and Certificate
  2. Class 12th Marksheet and Certificate
  3. Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  4. School/College Leaving Certificate (SLC/CLC)
  5. Caste/Category Certificate (for reservation claims)
  6. Recent Passport Size Photograph
  7. Signature (Scanned)
  8. Active Mobile Number and Email ID
  9. Aadhar Card

Selection Process

Admission to the Under Graduate programs at LNMU will be based on a Merit List. The university prepares the merit list based on the marks obtained by the candidate in their 10+2 / Intermediate examination and their category reservation. No entrance exam is conducted for regular UG admissions.

Step-by-Step Online Apply Process

Follow these simple steps to fill out the LNMU UG Admission form online:

  1. Visit the official admission portal of LNMU: lnmu.ac.in.
  2. Click on the "Online Portal for UG Admission 2026-30" link.
  3. Click on "New Registration" and fill in your basic details (Name, Mobile, Email) to generate login credentials.
  4. Login using your Application ID and Password.
  5. Fill in your personal details, educational qualifications, and college/subject preferences.
  6. Upload the required scanned documents (Photo, Signature, Marksheets).
  7. Pay the application fee online.
  8. Review the application form carefully and submit it.
  9. Take a printout of the final submitted form and fee receipt for future reference.
